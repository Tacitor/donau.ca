Who I am
I, Lukas Krampitz, am a Second-Year student at the University of Guelph. I am currently studying in a Bachelors of Computer Engineering program. I have a fascination and passion for computers and problem solving.

I have a love of programming and software as seen in my Catan game and my website projects. But I equally if not more love hardware and electronics, as seen in my Model M collection, and 8-bit full adder. A lot of my time is spent around computers. This extends to work, such as my time in the Geek Squad at Best Buy fixing computers. And it also extends to play, enjoying single player games like KSP and Hitman, and multiplayer games like Factorio, Civ VI, and Stardew Valley.

I enjoy travelling, and have been to quite a few countries including Estonia, Sweden, Finland, Norway, Italy, and most often of all Germany. I love biking on my Devinci Oslo, and take it all around the city. I also love downhill skiing and am particularly fond of glade skiing and getting lost in the trees.
