What is Lkrampitz.net?

Lkrampitz.net is the personal website of Lukas Krampitz. It is the home of many projects. One such project is the webserver that is hosting this very page. Another project was designing and creating the front-end of the static website too. Lkrampitz.net also showcases quite a few other personal projects too. One such project is the restoration, cleaning, and modification of IBM Model M and M122 keyboards. Lkrampitz.net is also home to many other miscellanies things such as travel pictures. The light house on the left is found on the German island of Amrum.
