This is a little game that I made with a friend of mine. It was the final summative project for TEJ4M, my Grade 12 Computer Enigeering course. The game is based off of the popular and well known game of Minesweeper, with an added multiplayer twist. The game we made together is written in Java, and makes use of Sockets to allow for the online multiplayer fuctionality. We split up the work fairly evenly. I was incharge of making the main menu, and its interactions with sub menus and the main game. I was also responsible for writing the netcode, and ensuring 2 computers could connect to play the game. My partner Ehsan was responsible for writing the main game and all the fuctionality it needed.

The main portion of the game has two small Minesweeper boards next to eachother. Each 10x10 board is assigned to one player. Each player gets to take their turn one after the other. A turn consists of either scouting a tile or placing a mine on your oppoments board. A player can flag as many tiles as they wish in a given turn, placing flags goes not count as taking your turn.
